My Favorite CMSC Subject

Currently, my favorite CMSC subject is CMSC 22: Object Oriented Programming. This is because I enjoyed learning the basic concepts and principles of OOP which are essential to software development. Understanding the pillars of OOP as well as how they work together was a long but fun process. More importantly, I had fun incorporating those essentials in creating mini game applications with user interface and animations through the platform Java FX. Creating my own simple mini game application using the knowledge I learned in this course was a fun and fulfilling experience.

My Favorite GE Subject

ARTS 1: Critical Perspectives in the Arts, which I took when I was still in my 1st year, is my favorite GE course. At first, I thought that the course would just require us to submit artworks and essays on how we see a certain art however, that is not the case. The course piqued my interest as it discussed how relevant the arts is in the society. I liked how it doesn't just revolve on the aesthetics of art but also on the discursive nature of art.
